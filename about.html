About Me

I am interested in all things tech. My primary focus programming up to this point has been Embedded Systems/Arduino/IoT/LED related projects. Mostly involving esp8266 and esp32 microcontrollers. My projects have evolved to the point where I am making my own custom WiFi and Bluetooth enabled microcontrollers with built in accelerometers, gyroscopes, IR receivers, and more. In addition to the microcontroller projects, I also work with depth cameras like the Microsoft Azure Kinect and programs like Touchdesigner making interactive art installation for events and music festivals.
